Value vs. Industrial Grade Pallets: Which Should You Choose?

The quick differences, when to use each, and how to choose based on load, handling, and budget.

Quick comparison

Value Pallet — $15

Budget-friendly used 48×40 composite pallet for simple, routine use. Normal cosmetic wear (minor, non-structural chips) is expected.

Industrial Grade — $30

Heavy-duty used 48×40 composite pallet for higher loads and frequent handling. A cost-saving alternative to new pallets often listed near $155.

How to choose

  • Weight & handling: Choose Industrial for heavier items or frequent forklift cycles.
  • Budget: Choose Value when savings is the priority and loads are lighter.
  • Consistency: Both options are 48×40 composite with dependable fit in standard racking.
