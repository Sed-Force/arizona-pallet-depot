Pickup vs. Delivery

Value vs. Industrial Grade

Value Pallet ($15/unit): Affordable, used 48×40 composite pallets with normal wear. Great for budget-conscious buyers.

Industrial Grade Pallet ($30/unit): Heavy-duty used 48×40 composite pallets, reliable for thousands of pounds. Comparable new pallets often cost $155+.
